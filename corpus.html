Corpus

The corpus is in active development. As editions are completed, they will be shared here. A future version of the site will also provide resources for querying the corpus.

Currently, editions are published as XML TEI files. Note that the transcriptions contain some characters that are best viewed with the Junicode font.

13th-century Old French Bible

The 13th-century Bible generally appears spread across two volumes, with the first covering Genesis—Psalms and the second Proverbs—Revelation.
