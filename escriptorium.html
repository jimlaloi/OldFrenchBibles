eScriptorium

Members of the research team use eScriptorium software as part of our transcription process. To open the Old French Bibles project in eScriptorium, click here. You will need login credentials, and our version of this tool is not available to the public. Note that eScriptorium works best in Firefox, and may have issues in Chrome.

We are currently experiencing technical issues with our primary eScriptorium URL. If you are unable to log in at the above link, try our backup link here.

You can learn more about the eScriptorium software project at its GitLab page.
